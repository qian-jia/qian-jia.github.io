About Me

Hello and welcome! I am a PhD candidate in Management Information Systems at Nanjing University, China. Currently, I am also a visiting PhD student at the University of Texas at Dallas, USA.

My primary focus lies in AI technologies and the optimization of enterprise decisions. Lately, my research has been directed towards addressing decision problems in information system security management and the digital grocery ecosystem. I am excited about the potential impact of my work in these areas.

Please check my CV for more info.

Interests
  • Information System Security
  • Decision Making Under Uncertainty
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Business Analytics
